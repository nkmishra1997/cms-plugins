Event Details

STUDENT PROFESSIONAL AWARENESS CONFERENCE (S-PAC) provides an opportunity for direct interaction between students and industrial professionals. This great exposure brings real life opportunities to the forefront for the students. The Interactive sessions help the students to see the world of opportunities, through the eyes of experienced people. It surely is a great learning curve for the students to know which career path suits them the best.
