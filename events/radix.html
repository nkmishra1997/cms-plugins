About Radix

RADIX, the most awaited robotic based event is back in Troika 2017. This phenomenal event provides an opportunity to people interested in the field of robotics to showcase their talents. The event consists of almost all aspects of robotics- the ultimate fusion of hardware and code.

Welcome to the ultimate robotic challenge where you will get a sneak peek of the Age Of The Robots. So grab your tools, fuse code and hardware, and meet us in the arena.
