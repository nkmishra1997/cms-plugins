About Mist

Imagine yourself lost in a world of wonders, where the way to your destination is covered with a thick impregnable mist. All you have as your guide is a small light, indeed a very bright one, Wisdom. Only the ones who can match their limitless knowledge with unparalleled speed can clear this mist and shape their path towards victory.

Such a prospect intrigues you, doesn't it? Join us, be a part of the most spectacular quizzing event this year has in store for you. Welcome to 'Mist'.

Event Details

This event invites scholarly individuals from all around the world to participate in the most spectacular quiz of the year. It is an online event and will be conducted through an online portal.
